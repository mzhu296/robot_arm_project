Our Main Goal:

The main goal of our project is to construct a robotic in a more cost effective way than more expensive models.

We plan to do this by 3D printing the outer shell and joints while self-coding the motors and using wire connections

Funtions of the Arm:

Our mechanical arm has accomplished these goals, similar to a more expensive model:

- Carry a large amount of weight without breaking

- Use computer vision to recognize the objects it interacts with and adjust accordingly

- Have full free complete motion, no restrictions

- Have adjustable speed and controls

- Have easily swapable parts in case of emergencies
